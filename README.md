# ClearCare
**AI-Powered Health Literacy & Navigation Platform**

> Harvard Health Systems Hackathon 2026 | Team Project
>
> **Challenges Addressed: #8 · #10 · #5**

---

## 📋 Project Description

> *What if you finally received your diagnosis — but had no idea what it meant?*

ClearCare is an AI-powered health literacy platform that turns complex medical documents into plain, understandable language — instantly. Millions of patients receive lab results or diagnoses they can't interpret, leading to delayed care and poor decisions. ClearCare bridges that gap.

Beyond translation, ClearCare matches users to trusted, evidence-backed health resources, cutting through the noise of 10,000+ available tools. An AI chatbot answers health questions, busts myths, and guides users to the right care — 24/7. Available on web and mobile, in multiple languages, for everyone.

---

## 🎯 Challenges Addressed

- **Challenge #8 — Health Literacy** *(Primary)*
  Empowering individuals to understand their own health conditions, recognize symptoms, and make informed decisions.

- **Challenge #10 — Breaking Communication Barriers** *(Integrated Feature)*
  AI translation of medical jargon into simple language with multilingual support.

- **Challenge #5 — Too Much Information** *(Integrated Feature)*
  Intelligent matching of users to reliable, evidence-backed apps and health resources.

## 🏗️ App Structure & Pages

ClearCare is organized into 6 core sections, accessible via web and mobile. Each section is designed with a specific user need in mind.

---

### 1. 🏠 Home / Dashboard

The first screen the user sees after login. Designed to give a personalized, at-a-glance view of the user's health activity and recommendations.

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Personalized Feed** | Displays health tips and content tailored to the user's profile, conditions, and past interactions. |
| **Quick Actions** | One-tap access to the Medical Translator, Chatbot, and Resource Finder. |
| **Recent Activity** | Shows the user's last translated documents, saved resources, and chatbot conversations. |
| **Health Score Card** | A simple visual summary of the user's health literacy progress over time. |

---

### 2. 📄 Medical Translator

The core feature of ClearCare. Users upload or paste any medical document — lab results, diagnostic reports, prescriptions, or discharge summaries — and the AI instantly produces a clear, plain-language explanation.

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Document Upload** | Supports PDF, image (photo of paper), and text paste as input formats. |
| **AI Explanation** | Breaks down the document section by section with simple language, avoiding medical jargon. |
| **Term Glossary** | Every medical term is highlighted and linked to a simple definition the user can tap/click. |
| **Multilingual Output** | Translated explanations available in multiple languages to support non-English speakers. |
| **Ask About This Report** | Users can ask follow-up questions about their specific document directly in-context. |

---

### 3. 🤖 AI Health Chatbot

A conversational AI assistant available 24/7 to answer health questions, bust myths, and guide users toward appropriate care. Designed for both patients and caregivers.

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Symptom Q&A** | Users describe symptoms in plain language and receive clear, safe guidance — including when to see a doctor. |
| **Myth Busting** | The chatbot identifies and corrects common health misconceptions with evidence-based information. |
| **Caregiver Mode** | A special mode tailored for family members or caregivers supporting a patient. |
| **Source Citations** | Every response includes references to trusted medical sources (WHO, NHS, PubMed, etc.). |
| **Escalation Prompts** | Detects urgent situations and clearly directs users to emergency services or a physician. |

---

### 4. 🔍 Resource & App Finder

Addresses the overwhelming number of health apps and resources available online. ClearCare's AI filters and recommends only trusted, evidence-backed tools based on the user's specific needs.

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Smart Matching** | AI matches users to apps and resources based on their health profile, conditions, and preferences. |
| **Evidence Rating** | Each recommendation shows an evidence quality score — how well-supported it is by clinical research. |
| **Filters** | Users can filter by: free vs. paid, language, condition type, and platform (iOS/Android/Web). |
| **Community Reviews** | Verified user reviews from people with similar conditions to ensure real-world relevance. |
| **Saved Resources** | Users can bookmark and organize their preferred apps and resources. |

---

### 5. 📚 Learn Hub

A structured educational space offering accessible, accurate health content. Designed to progressively build the user's health literacy over time.

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Condition Library** | Simple, clear explanations of common diseases, conditions, and medical procedures. |
| **Bite-Sized Articles** | Short, readable articles on symptoms, treatments, medications, and prevention. |
| **Literacy Quizzes** | Interactive short quizzes to help users test and improve their health knowledge. |
| **Myth vs. Fact Cards** | Swipeable cards debunking popular health myths with cited facts. |
| **Caregiver Guides** | Dedicated guides for family members and caregivers supporting patients. |

---

### 6. 👤 Profile & Personalization

Enables the entire platform to adapt to the individual user. The more information provided, the more personalized and relevant every feature becomes.

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Health Profile** | User inputs age, gender, existing conditions, and medications to personalize all features. |
| **Language Preference** | Sets the user's preferred language for all content and translations. |
| **Accessibility Settings** | Font size, color contrast, and text-to-speech options for users with disabilities. |
| **Notifications** | Optional reminders for medication, check-ups, or daily health tips. |
| **Privacy & Data Control** | Full transparency on data usage with options to export or delete personal data. |

---

## 👥 Target Users

- Patients who receive medical reports and struggle to understand them
- Caregivers and family members supporting a person with a health condition
- Individuals in low health-literacy communities or non-English speaking populations
- People overwhelmed by the number of available health apps and resources

---

## 💡 Why ClearCare Works

- Solves a real, measurable problem: poor health literacy leads to worse outcomes and higher costs
- Integrates three hackathon challenges into one seamless, unified product
- AI at the core — not as a gimmick, but as a genuine enabler of accessibility
- Multilingual by design — inclusive of underserved and non-English-speaking populations
- Available as web and mobile, maximizing reach across all demographics
- Evidence-first approach — all recommendations and content are tied to trusted medical sources
